二. 递归遍历文件树：&nbsp;

Glob 函数扫描非常准确，并且会自动排好顺序:

$path = '..';&nbsp;

function get_filetree($path){&nbsp;

$tree = array();&nbsp;

foreach(glob($path.'/*') as $single){&nbsp;

if(is_dir($single)){&nbsp;

$tree = array_merge($tree,get_filetree($single));&nbsp;

}&nbsp;

else{&nbsp;

$tree[] = $single;&nbsp;

}&nbsp;

}&nbsp;

return $tree;&nbsp;

}&nbsp;
